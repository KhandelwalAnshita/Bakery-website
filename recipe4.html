Pinata Cake

Step 1 Melt 50 g Dark chocolate. Add the butter and mix to ensure that the butter also melts.

Step 2 Add in the buttermilk and mix well to ensure you have a homogeneous mixture ready.

Step 3 Add the sugar and mix well.Blend in the baking soda and set aside for 5 minutes.

Step 4 Sift together the baking powder, flour, cocoa powder and set aside.Add the vanilla essence to the butter milk mixture.

Step 5 Mix in the dry ingredients till you have a lump free batter ready with you.Do not overmix the batter.

Step 6 Pour into a prepared 7‚Äù baking mould.Microwave for 4 minutes or bake in a preheated oven at 180*C for 35 minutes.Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes and then demould.

Step 7 Meanwhile, melt together the cream and the chocolate.Mix well to get a smooth consistency. Add the butter and mix well.

Step 8 Refrigerate the ganache for 30 minutes.Whisk the cooled ganache till light and fluffy.
